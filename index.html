Italian Frittata Recipe

A delicious Italian-style omelette that's perfect for any meal. This classic frittata combines beaten eggs with Italian herbs, cheese, and vegetables for an authentic taste of Italy.

Preparation time

  • Total Time: Approximately 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

1.

Prepare the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, chopped basil, salt, and pepper until well combined.

2.

Heat the pan: Place a non-stick, oven-safe skillet over medium heat and add olive oil.

3.

Add vegetables: Sauté the cherry tomatoes and bell peppers for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.

4.

Cook the frittata: Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables, tilting the pan to ensure even distribution. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the edges begin to set.

5.

Finish in oven: Transfer the skillet to a preheated oven (350°F/180°C) and bake for 8-10 minutes until the frittata is fully set and lightly golden on top.
